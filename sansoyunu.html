Welcome to the game. This is a game of chance. First, select the amount of money you want to bet below. Then, click the "Start with this amount" button. Clicking the button will give you access to the buttons on the screen. Of the nine buttons, you must select only the green ones. For each green button you select, you will win 1.2 times the amount you bet. However, if you click a red button, all the money you bet will be transferred to the cash register. You can end the game whenever you want after you start. Good luck.
